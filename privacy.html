Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2025

Privacy First: Your health data stays on your device. We don't collect, store, or share your personal lens or health information.

1. Information We Do NOT Collect

Lens Tracker App is designed with privacy in mind. We do NOT collect:

  • Lens prescription information
  • Personal health data
  • Usage patterns or statistics
  • Photos of prescriptions
  • Contact information
  • Location data
  • Device identifiers for tracking

2. Local Data Storage

All your data is stored locally on your device using:

  • Local Database: Lens records, settings, and preferences
  • Device Photos: Prescription photos (premium feature) stored in app's private container
  • UserDefaults: App settings and configuration
  • App Group: Shared data between main app and widget

3. Third-Party Services

RevenueCat (Subscription Management)

For premium subscriptions, we use RevenueCat which may collect:

  • Anonymous subscription status
  • App Store transaction information
  • Device type for subscription validation

RevenueCat's Privacy Policy: https://www.revenuecat.com/privacy

4. Permissions We Request

Notifications

  • Purpose: Remind you to change lenses
  • Data: Only scheduling information, no content sent externally

Photos (Premium Feature)

  • Purpose: Save prescription photos
  • Storage: Locally in app container only
  • Access: Never shared or uploaded

5. Widget Data

The home screen widget displays your lens information by reading locally stored data. No information is transmitted outside your device for widget functionality.

6. Data Sharing

We do not share your data with anyone. Your health information never leaves your device except for:

  • Standard iOS backup to iCloud (if you have enabled it)
  • Device-to-device transfer when setting up a new device

7. Data Security

Your data is protected by:

  • iOS app sandboxing
  • Local encryption for sensitive data
  • No network transmission of personal data
  • App Group security for widget communication

8. Your Rights

Since all data is stored locally on your device, you have complete control:

  • Access: All data is viewable within the app
  • Delete: Use "Reset All Data" in Settings or delete the app
  • Export: Data stays on your device; no external export needed

9. Children's Privacy

Our app is rated 4+ and safe for all ages. We do not knowingly collect any information from users of any age, as all data remains local to the device.

10. Changes to Privacy Policy

We will notify users of any material changes to this privacy policy through the app and update the "Last updated" date above.

11. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, contact us at: lenstrackerapp@gmail.com
